Privacy Policy for EbillFast

Last updated: 2026-01-14

EbillFast respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles user data.

1. Data Collection

EbillFast does not require user accounts and does not collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number for the offline-first version.

All data entered into the app, such as bills, payment records, attachments, PDFs, and images, is stored locally on the user’s device unless optional cloud features are enabled in a future version.

2. Advertising & Advertising ID

EbillFast displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may collect and use device identifiers, including the Android Advertising ID, in order to display and measure advertisements.

The Advertising ID is managed by the Android operating system. Users can reset or delete it at any time from their device settings.

The developer of EbillFast does not have direct access to the Advertising ID and does not store it.

3. Subscriptions & Payments

EbillFast offers optional premium subscriptions that remove advertisements.

All payments and subscription management are handled exclusively by Google Play Billing.

The app developer does not collect, store, or process payment information such as credit card or debit card details.

4. Data Safety

No personal data is sold by the developer. Advertising-related data may be processed by Google AdMob according to Google's privacy policies.

Users can remove local data by deleting bills, removing backups, or uninstalling the app.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Google Play Billing (subscriptions)

6. Children’s Privacy

EbillFast is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer through the support details available on the Google Play listing or the official website.
