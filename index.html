FAQ

Does EbillFast make payments inside the app?

No. EbillFast helps you organize and prepare payments safely, then hands off the payment details to your banking app.

Can I import bills from PDF or camera scan?

Yes. You can import PDFs, share a bill into the app, or scan paper bills with the camera and review the extracted fields before saving.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Core bill management is offline-first. Internet is only needed for ads, subscriptions, banking-app handoff, and optional cloud features.

Can I restore my data on a new phone?

Yes. You can export a backup ZIP and import it on another device.
